name: rtl-product-qa
description: Use after UI changes to review Arabic RTL product quality, responsive behavior, and role-specific navigation for the engineering office app.
---

Review the running app as an Arabic RTL business tool, not as a marketing landing page.

Check:

1. Layout direction, sidebars, tables, forms, icons, and alignment work naturally in RTL.
2. Text fits inside cards, tables, buttons, nav items, and mobile screens.
3. Manager, engineer, and accountant navigation show only appropriate modules.
4. Financial fields are absent for engineers, not merely visually de-emphasized.
5. Project/task workflows are fast to scan and update.
6. Mobile PWA flow remains usable for engineers in the field.
7. Empty, loading, error, offline, and permission-denied states exist.
